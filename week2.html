GPlates Cesium

GPlates Cesium is an interactive website that allows you to explore the movement of Earth's tectonic plates at different geological times. You can choose to view the site in either a 3D globe, 2D Map, or in their flat layed out "Columbus" setting. It provides a data set which allows you to view/visualize Earth's major post-Pangea rift systems and to compare the changes in full rift velocity.
